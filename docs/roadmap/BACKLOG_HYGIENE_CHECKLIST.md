# Backlog Hygiene Checklist

## Purpose

Use this checklist during backlog cleanup and recurring roadmap review to prevent entropy.

## Hygiene checks

- Are all active items present in the canonical roadmap set?
- Are there any ID collisions or reused IDs?
- Are any items duplicated under slightly different names?
- Do active items have current `Priority class`, `Queue rank`, and `Target horizon` values?
- Are architecture-dependent items still aligned to the architecture master?
- Are grouped execution opportunities still current?
- Are blocked items explicitly marked as blocked, deferred, or frozen instead of silently stalling?
- Are external-system-dependent items backed by a verified documentation pack?
- Do summary surfaces still match status truth?
- Are stale or superseded supporting docs clearly marked or migrated?

## Rule

A backlog with weak hygiene becomes misleading even if individual items are well written.
