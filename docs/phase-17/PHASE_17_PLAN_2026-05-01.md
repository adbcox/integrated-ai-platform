## Charter

Phase 17 is built on the foundation that Phase 16 cross-index work made possible. Driven by the stack audit (logical), capability probes (per-tool), and physical-plane audit (hardware/network/storage topology).

The phase opens with three foundation audits (17.A, 17.B, 17.C) that produce templates and findings used by every other deliverable. Tier 2 deliverables apply those templates to specific architectural questions left open by Phase 16. Tier 3 picks up the local AI stack work that was Phase 17's original scope before D#19 (stack-level audit at phase boundaries) reordered it. Tier 4 carries over four Phase 16 items that depend on Tier 1/2 output. Tier 5 codifies the doctrine and consolidates intake findings from the 2026-04-30→05-01 article-review window.

This plan does NOT execute any Tier 1-4 deliverables. Each is its own deliverable prompt, scoped after this plan lands and is operator-reviewed.
